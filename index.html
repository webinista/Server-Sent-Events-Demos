Server-sent events

Server-sent event demos

Server-sent events are a way of polling a specific URL, and retrieving a stream of text data from the server.

If these demos do not work in your browser, upgrade or switch. The latest versions of Opera, Chrome/Chromium, Safari, and Firefox support these features.
